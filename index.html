Dexter "Buddy Bear" Dog

Dexter was the first dog I could call my own. I had always dreamed of owning a golden retriever, so when Dexter came into my life, it was like a dream come true. We were like father and son, and could do no wrong in each others' eyes.

Clementine "Tiny" Dog

A rescue intended to replace a cherished friend, Clementine chose ours to be her "fur-ever" home instead of the one she was chosen for. Neurotic and ferocious, we needed to earn her trust before we knew how much love she had to offer.

Lincoln "Stinkin'" Dog

Changing lives and changing life companions brought this little bundle of spunk and charm into the family, effortlessly wriggling his way into everyone's hearts.

The Three Musketeers

Overcoming significant differences in age, size, temperament and life experience, Dexter, Tiny and Lincoln became fast friends, enjoying many adventures together.

One Last Walk

Sadly, illness took Dexter from us much too soon. Beloved of everyone he met, the hole in our hearts can never be filled.

Tiny and Stink

In the wake of Dexter's passing, Tiny and Lincoln embrace each other, becoming a dynamic duo.

A New Baby

Time passes and a new arrival comes, this time in a package befitting the size of the challenges she will present.

Hlína "Gomper" Dog

A natural guardian, Hlína all too quickly grows to establish her dominance over the pack and our hearts.

Three Musketeers Ride Again

In time, our wounds mend, the bond of family is reborn, and the Three Musketeers ride again.

Unconditional Love

Life teaches us that no love is without its share of trials and tribulations. Owning a dog means learning that the love you have to give will be unconditionally returned.
